Massage Therapy consists primarily of “Hands-On” manipulation of soft tissues of the body, specifically the muscles, connective tissue, tendons, ligaments, and joints for the purpose of optimizing health.
Massage Therapy is a mainstream treatment approach, which is growing in demand and popularity. Massage Therapy treatment has a therapeutic effect on the body by acting on the muscular, nervous, and circulatory systems.
Due to massage Therapy, the physical function can be developed, maintain, and improved. Physical dysfunction, pain, and the effects of stress can be relieved or prevented.
