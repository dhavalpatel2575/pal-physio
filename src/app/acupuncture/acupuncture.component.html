Acupuncture is a form of alternative medicine and key component of traditional Chinese Medicine in which thin needles are inserted into the body in specific parts called Acupuncture Points. The needles may be stimulated by movement or an electrical stimulators. Acupuncture helps the body to release of natural pain inhibiting chemicals such as endorphins.

Acupuncture is one of the most widely used alternative medicines throughout the world. Acupuncture is generally safe when done by appropriately trained practitioners using clean needle technique and single-use needles. Acupuncture is generally used only in combination with other forms of treatment.

This Treatment is growing in popularity and currently used to treat condition like…

  • Most often used to attempt for “PAIN RELIEF”.
  • Osteoarthritis
  • Headaches and migraines
  • Anxiety & depression
  • Chronic back pain/ Neck pain
  • Sleep disturbance (Insomnia)
  • Post operative pain
  • Carpal tunnel symptoms
