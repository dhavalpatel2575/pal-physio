Custom Foot Orthotic is a prescription medical device that is added to the shoe, “shoe insert” that is molded to your feet. They can only be ordered by medical professionals after examination of your feet, your gait, and an analysis of any pain you may be experiencing.

BENEFITS OF CUSTOM FOOT ORTHOTICS

  1. Raising and supporting arches.
  2. Custom Foot Orthotics are designed to realign the feet and ankles and attempts to restore the full function of the feet.
  3. They also act to make activities such as walking, running or standing more efficient.
  4. Redistribute pressure on the bottom of the foot to relieve pain from excessive pressure on sensitive spots on your feet.

CUSTOM FOOT ORTHOTICS ARE USED TO TREAT CONDITIONS SUCH AS…

  • Planter Facilities                             
  • Achilles Tendonitis
  • Heel Spur Syndrome                     
  • Shin Splints                 
  • Sciatica                                              
  • Lower Back Pain
  • Metatarsalgia
  • Bunions
