EXERCISE PROGRAM

At PAL Physio & Rehab, we believe in an active approach to improving physical health and performance. Exercises will bring a wide range of physical and mental health benefits. A customized exercise program developed by a Registered Physiotherapist / Chiropractor is a great way to recover and stay fit.

BENEFITS OF EXERCISES

  • Exercise plays a vital role in building & maintaining strong muscles and bones.  Exercises,  to build bone density when you are younger, helping to prevent osteoporosis in later life.
  • Exercise helps people rebuild strength and movement in parts of their body after an injury.
  • Exercise can help people to manage pain and prevent permanent damage and recurring problems.
  • Regular exercises improve flexibility and balance.

To enhance your recovery, exercises are performed in the clinic and a home exercise program is individually prepared for a client.

While designing your home exercise program we will consider,

  • Your short term / long term goals.
  • We will plan the logical progression of exercises.
  • Start cautiously and progress slowly.

Home exercises are a very important part of your recovery process so, During your first follow up visit to the clinic, we will provide you Hands-out with pictures/diagrams, description, and prescription of exercises(e.g. how many repetitions, numbers of the set to perform in a day) the hand-outs will also include the precautions to follow during exercises.
